Mission

We are a non-profit organization aiming to raise literacy rates worldwide through children’s games by enlisting corporate sponsorship. We strive to create a world where every child has the opportunity to learn, stay connected to the world, be productive, and thrive.

Core Values

Responsibility: We want to build an environment that supports the development of children’s education worldwide.
Respect: We have for the rights, differences, and dignity of others.
Honesty and integrity: In dealing with all members of the community we value transparency.

Positioning Statement

Our service educates two communities simultaneously: the kids who are learning by playing the games and helping kids all over the world, who will improve their literacy levels from the educational tools funded by the games.
