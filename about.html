Mission

We are a non-profit organization aiming to raise literacy rates worldwide through children’s games by enlisting corporate sponsorship.

Core Values

Responsable: We want to build an environment in which mutual trust can develop and that allows development of children’s education worldwide Respect for the rights, differences, and dignity of others Honesty and integrity in dealing with all members of the community

Positioning Statement

Our service educates two communities simultaneously: the kids learning by playing the games, knowing they are assisting others and the kids who will improve their literacy levels from the educational tools funded by the games.
